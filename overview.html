Overview

What is Keywords Checker?

Keywords Checker is a free browser-based tool for Amazon sellers. It analyzes how well your product listing title covers a set of target keywords — showing exact match types, missing words, and search volume coverage.

How it works

Three steps, instant results.

1

Add your keywords

Paste keywords one per line, or import a .xlsx file from Amazon Brand Analytics, Helium 10, or any keyword research tool. Search volume is supported and used for coverage analysis.

2

Paste your listing title

Type or paste your Amazon product title in the text input area. The analysis runs instantly as you type — no button to press.

3

Read the results

Each keyword gets a color-coded status: Full Exact, Exact, Phrase, Broad, or Not Found. The preview panel highlights matched words directly in your title text.

Match types explained

Keywords Checker uses four levels of matching, from strongest to weakest.

⭐ Full Exact

The keyword phrase appears verbatim in your title. Exact character-for-character match (case-insensitive). The strongest possible coverage signal.

Exact

All keyword words appear in sequence with lemmatization applied. Word forms are recognized: "mats" matches "mat", "crawling" matches "crawl", "babies" matches "baby".

Phrase

All words appear in the correct order, but up to 2 other words may appear between them. The keyword's word order is preserved, but the phrase isn't contiguous.

Broad

All content words are present anywhere in the title, in any order. Common stop words (a, the, for, with…) are ignored. Weakest match but still counts as coverage.

Not Found

The keyword has no coverage in the title. Important words from this keyword are listed in the Missing Words panel so you can address them.

Key features

Built for real Amazon listing work.

Search Volume Coverage panel — see what % of your total keyword search volume is covered by your title, broken down by match type.
Missing Words panel — click any missing word to copy it instantly. Know exactly what to add to your title.
Excel import (.xlsx) — paste directly from Helium 10, Amazon Brand Analytics, or any spreadsheet. Keyword column is auto-detected.
Export to Excel — download your full keyword analysis with match statuses and meta columns.
Filter & sort — filter keywords by match status, sort by volume, alphabetically, or by import order.
Duplicate detection — duplicate keywords in your list are flagged automatically.
Title Case button — convert your listing title to proper title case in one click.
Character counter — tracks your title length in real time, including selection count.
Dark & Light theme — switch between themes for comfortable use in any environment.
No server, no account — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
