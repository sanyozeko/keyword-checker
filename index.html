Match Types

How matching works

⭐ Full Exact

Keyword appears verbatim in your title. The strongest match — the exact phrase is present with no changes.

Exact

All words present in the same order, with lemmatization. Word forms are recognized — "mats" matches "mat", "crawling" matches "crawl".

Phrase

All words appear in order with up to 2 other words between them. The keyword structure is preserved but not contiguous.

Broad

All content words are present anywhere in the title, in any order. Stop words (a, the, for…) are ignored.

Not Found

The keyword is not covered at all. Consider adding the missing words to your listing title.
